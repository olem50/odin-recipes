Baguette

Baguette. Nature's perfect breakfast.

Baguette with ham, cheese, cucumber and tomato.


Ingredients:

  • Baguette
  • Cheese
  • Sliced ham
  • Cucumber
  • Tomato

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 225C.
  2. Cut up cucumber and tomato in slices
  3. Put the baguette in the oven at 225C for 12 minutes.
  4. Cut the baguette in half.
  5. Add slices of cheese.
  6. Add slices of ham.
  7. Add cucumber and tomato.
  8. Ready to be served!
