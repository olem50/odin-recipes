Pizza. Nature's perfect food.

Pizza with chicken and tomato.


Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Chicken
  • Tomato
  • Grated cheese

Steps:

  1. Cut up chicken into cubes.
  2. Fry the chicken in a pan.
  3. Cut up tomato into half slices.
  4. Spread sauce evenly over the dough.
  5. Add chicken and tomato slices on top of the dough.
  6. Bake in oven at 225C for 15 minutes.
  7. Cut pizza into slices.
  8. Ready to be served!
