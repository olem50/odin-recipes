Lasagna

Lasagna. Nature's perfect food.

Pasta with tomato sauce, minced meat and cheese.


Ingredients:

  • Flat pasta
  • Spiced tomato sauce
  • Minced meat
  • Grated cheese

Steps:

  1. Fry the minced meat in a pan.
  2. Add sauce to pan.
  3. Put flat pasta on the bottom of a dish.
  4. Evenly add some of the meat and sauce on top.
  5. Put a slice of pasta on top, add the meat and sauce.
  6. Repeat step 5 until the meat and sauce is used up.
  7. Put grated cheese on the top.
  8. Bake in oven at 200C for 20 minutes.
  9. Ready to be served!
